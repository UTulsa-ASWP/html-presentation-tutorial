## How to use this script

This walks the slide deck one slide at a time. Each slide has a few moves:

- **Note** — what is on the slide, and what to look at first.
- **Say** — read it aloud, or put it in your own words.
- **Ask** — toss a question to the room; **Expected Student Responses** lists the answers students usually give.
- **Teacher Do** — what to write, draw, or point to on the board or slide.

You do **not** need a computer-science or AI background to teach this — every new
term is defined the first time it appears. Mix the moves as each slide needs (a
slide can have several **Say** or **Ask** blocks), and keep meaning checks short
and frequent.

**Timing:** note any natural day / Part splits here — e.g. "Slides 1–6 = Part 1."
